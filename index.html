ABOUT

The Story Behind Sanki Safar

Sanki Safar (which roughly translates to "Crazy Journey" in Hindi) started as a personal diary of my travels and has grown into a community of wanderers seeking authentic experiences.

Through this blog, I share stories that move—tales of people met, cultures discovered, and the transformative power of stepping outside our comfort zones.
